Full 5-fold StratifiedKFold repeated across 20 seeds (100 fold-model runs per config, 500 total for GBSA alone plus 25 seeds × 5 folds for LGB). Handles the near-zone subset adaptively (adjusts n_splits based on minority class size). No data leakage.

Conclusion & Recommendations

Your four-notebook solution is the most complete, best-documented, and most pedagogically valuable in the set. wids-n20 is the strongest on pure modelling performance. Here is a concrete improvement roadmap:

✓ What You Did Well

  • Best-in-class EDA — systematic, statistical, actionable
  • Outstanding documentation — every decision justified
  • Clean preprocessing pipeline — no leakage, robust scaler
  • Good coverage of KM/cumulative incidence visualisation
  • Correct survival analysis framing with monotonicity enforcement
  • Well-structured four-notebook architecture with clean handoffs
  • Dual feature selection (correlation + mutual information)

→ Highest-Impact Improvements

  • Replace RSF with GradientBoostingSurvivalAnalysis (GBSA)
  • Add 5-fold StratifiedKFold with multiple seeds — biggest score gain
  • Add effective ETA feature: dist / (closing_speed + radial_growth)
  • Add zone flag features: zone_critical, zone_warning, zone_safe
  • Hard-assign prob_72h = 1.0 for near-zone, 0.0 for far-zone fires
  • Add IPCW-weighted LGB classifier head for 24h and 48h horizons
  • Implement regime-conditioned calibration (stable vs. sparse near-zone)
  • Add Optuna hyperparameter search in notebook 04
