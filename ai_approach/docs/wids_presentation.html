Lessons Learned

🎓 For AI Users

  • Be specific with requirements
  • Provide context & examples
  • Iterate and refine outputs
  • Validate AI suggestions
  • Use appropriate modes/tools
  • Document as you go

🤖 For AI Development

  • Specialized modes are powerful
  • Context awareness is critical
  • Explain reasoning clearly
  • Handle large files efficiently
  • Support iterative workflows
  • Maintain code quality standards
Key Takeaway: The best results come from treating AI as a collaborative partner, not just a tool. Clear communication, domain expertise, and iterative refinement create powerful synergies.
